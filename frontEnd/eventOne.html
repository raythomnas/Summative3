Event Description

Make your life more fun.

Our Paint Parties are a super fun and creative way to unwind and try something new. Join us in our art studio to paint, socialise, unwind and find your creativity. We’ll provide all the arty materials you’ll need and show you, step by step, how to create your own painting. And, you don’t need any experience. Our Paint Parties are created especially for people who've never held a paint brush or reckon they can't even draw a stick figure. If you think you're not creative or artistic - we're here to help you unleash your inner artist and have fun doing it.
Any skill level welcome. You're free to follow along and paint any of the six featured paintings or go freestyle and paint whatever you like.

What to expect:
3 hours of painting fun in a chilled, creative studio environment. (Includes a short break in the middle to enjoy your complimentary refreshments and snacks).
Your finished painting to take home and hang on your wall so you can say "Wow I painted that" every time you see it.
