Event Description

At the 2020 Business Intelligence Summit, you will explore the most recent trends in BI, the emerging role that BI plays in today's business environment, and the new technologies that help provide unparalleled insights into how your market is moving and evolving. With the help of our expert presenters, this Summit will help you examine all facets of the sector and gain expertise in analytics, machine learning, AI, data science, cloud, digitisation and IoT.

Being quick on the uptake of emerging technologies, and an increasing dependency on and growing usage of data are all vital for company growth and success in the modern, fast-paced and highly competitive business environment.

Look forward to two days of high-quality content that will show you how to best utilise your data when making critical decisions!.

WHY YOU SHOULD ATTEND:
- Gain insights from leading Kiwi organisations sharing their BI journey
- Learn and exchange best practices on how to succeed in nowadays world of data
- Harness your data to provide strategic value and to make business-critical decisions
